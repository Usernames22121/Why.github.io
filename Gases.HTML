Working Safely with Refrigerants

  • Only licensed professionals should handle refrigerants.
  • Always wear protective equipment (gloves, goggles, mask).
  • Ensure proper ventilation when working indoors.
  • Recover and recycle gases according to environmental regulations.
  • Never release refrigerant gases into the atmosphere.

By following these guidelines, we help protect both people and the environment.
